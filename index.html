Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What's your process for designing a website?

+

I start with understanding your brand, goals, and audience. Then I create detailed wireframes, design mockups, and iterate based on your feedback. Finally, I hand-code everything to ensure performance and seamless interactions.

How long does a project typically take?

+

Most projects take 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. Rush projects are available upon request. We maintain constant communication and provide regular updates throughout.

Do you offer revisions and support after launch?

+

Yes! All projects include 30 days of free support after launch. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your site optimized, secure, and updated.

What's included in your design services?

+

Complete package: strategy & discovery, UX/UI design, custom development, animations, SEO optimization, mobile responsiveness, and deployment. No templates—everything is custom.

What's your pricing?

+

Projects typically range from $5,000 to $25,000+ depending on scope and complexity. I offer custom quotes after understanding your needs. Let's chat about your vision.
